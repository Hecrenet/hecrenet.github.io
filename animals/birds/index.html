Birds

What are birds?

Many people consider birds to just be the tiny things that can take flight by flapping their wings like maniacs. This is false.

Birds are majestic winged creatures. Since birds are so diverse, this is the common definition that we use when talking about birds.

Common Powers

One common power of birds is the ability to make weird noises with their throats.

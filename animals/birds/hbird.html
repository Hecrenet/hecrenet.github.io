Controversy

As the greatest creature in the universe, there is much controversy to surround the Hecretary Bird.

Hector's House

Many would believe that the Hecretary Bird's head was photoshopped right out of Hector from Hector's House. This is false.

Hector's head is very similar to the Hecretary Bird's head, due to it being one of the Hecretary Bird's creations. Hector was one of its first creations, which is why Hector takes the appearance of a dog.

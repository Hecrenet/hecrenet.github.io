Who?

The Bearded Snaxe is notorius for its vicious beard. Foes also never expect it to take flight

Appearance

The Bearded Snaxe has the appearance of a common Snaxe with a beard. It is also very stylish and is seen wearing very refined accessories.

Personality Problem

All Bearded Snaxii have a split personality. One can usually predict the personality of the Snaxe, but no one can predict the personality of its beard.

The Snaxe

The Snaxe, if you may, of a Bearded Snaxe tend to be socially shy with other species, and prefer hanging around with each other.

For some unknown reasons, these Snaxii like to wage war agaisnt crows at local Costcos, which can lead to people thinking it's night already

When these Snaxii are not warriors, they prefer to just be there and not do anything. The conflict of their personality is what lands them in the moderately dangerous categoryr

They are also very stylish, and can be compared to Squidlings from Splatoon. Don't sue us Nintendo, we don't have any money.

The Beard

The beard is mysterious and unique to the Bearded Snaxii. It has a mind of it's own and it is are very agressive. So agressive they are, that the Bearded Snaxii have invented a special muzzle for their beards, but their laziness prevents them from using it.

It is rare to see a Bearded Snaxii that has a good relationship with their beard. Many of the beards of Bearded Snaxe like to forcefully lift their masters into the sky, which is why Bearded Snaxii are great warriors, since they know how to channel their anger.

Beards of Bearded Snaxii aren't afraid, even of being shaved off, mainly because they can't be shaved. (This is true for a female Bearded Snaxe)
